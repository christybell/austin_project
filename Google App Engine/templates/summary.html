Welcome to Vine & Vault!

We are four recent graduates of UT Austin's Data Analytics and Visualization Bootcamp and we developed this site to showcase our final project. Have a look around!


About our project: We chose to explore a Wine Reviews dataset from Wine Enthusiast magazine. We selected this topic because we're a group of wine enthusiasts but we're certainly no sommeliers. Since wine can be complicated and overwhelming, we wanted to create a fun and interactive way for beginners to discover new wines. With this idea in mind, we built a dashboard to recommend wines for a novice based on such things as price, rating, variety, country and province. We also built a machine learning model to see if we could train it to rate wine like an experienced sommelier.


Explore our dashboard or our GitHub repository.
